# Content & UX Guidelines

To ensure the educational material and AI responses are easily digestible for all users, the following guidelines must be applied to all text content and UI layouts:

## Writing Style
- Use short, concise sentences.
- Break down complex information using bullet points.
- Provide instructions and explanations in a clear, step-by-step format.

## Typography & Layout
- Ensure text is large and highly readable by default.
- Use generous line height and margin spacing to prevent visual clutter.
